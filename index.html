WEAPON CATEGORIES

RIFLES (Vandal, Phantom, Bulldog, Guardian)

Rifles are the most versatile weapons in Valorant. The Vandal offers one-tap potential at any range, while the Phantom excels in close-medium combat. The Bulldog is a budget-friendly option with a deadly alt-fire burst mode. The Guardian is a semi-auto rifle with high damage.

SMGS (Spectre, Stinger)

SMGs are perfect for eco rounds and close-range engagements. The Spectre is accurate while moving, and the Stinger has a high fire rate that can surprise enemies up close.

SHOTGUNS (Judge, Bucky)

Shotguns dominate tight corners and small sites. The Judge is fully automatic, while the Bucky's alt-fire provides a precise slug shot.
