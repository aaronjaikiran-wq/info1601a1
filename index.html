Travel FAQs

What is the best time to travel to Southeast Asia?

The ideal time is generally November to April during the dry season. Temperatures are pleasant and rainfall is minimal, making it perfect for sightseeing, beaches, and outdoor adventures.

Do I need travel insurance?

We strongly recommend comprehensive travel insurance covering medical emergencies, trip cancellation, and lost luggage. Many countries now require proof of travel insurance at the border.

How far in advance should I book flights?

For international flights, booking 3 to 6 months in advance typically yields the best prices. For popular destinations during peak season, booking even earlier is advisable.

What documents do I need for international travel?

You will generally need a valid passport with at least 6 months of remaining validity, any required visas, return flight confirmation, and proof of accommodation.
